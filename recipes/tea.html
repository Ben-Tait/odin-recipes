Kenyan Tea

Description

Kenyan tea is a warm and comforting drink usually made with milk, water, tea leaves, and sugar.

It is simple to make and is enjoyed at breakfast or during breaks.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Tea leaves
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil water in a sufuria or pot.
  2. Add tea leaves and let them simmer.
  3. Add milk and sugar.
  4. Let it boil for a few minutes.
  5. Strain into a cup and serve hot.
