Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft, fluffy, and quick to prepare for breakfast or a snack

They can be served with syrup, honey, or fruit.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and eggs, then whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly greased pan.
  4. Pour some batter onto the pan.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  6. Cook the other side and serve warm.
