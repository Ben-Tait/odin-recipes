Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a rich and comforting dish made with layers of pasta, minced meat, tomato sauce, and cheese.

It is great for family meals and can be prepared ahead of time before baking.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Minced beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Cook the onion, garlic, and minced beef in a pan.
  3. Add tomato sauce and let it simmer.
  4. Layer the sauce, lasagna sheets, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat the layers until the dish is full.
  6. Bake until the top is golden and bubbling.
  7. Let it cool slightly before serving.
